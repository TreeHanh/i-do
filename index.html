YOUR ALL IN ONE

I Do is a customizable app designed to help you plan and communicate with members of your wedding party, all in one place.

Make a Profile

Personalize your profile by adding important wedding information for everyone to see.

Invite Your Wedding Party

Easily find contacts from your phone, Facebook, and email to invite them to join the wedding party.

Assign Roles

Easily assign roles to your invites to help you plan and organize your wedding.

Messages

Don't worry about the hassle of getting everyone onto the same messaging platform. Whether they're on iOS, Android, or web, they'll be in the loop.

Our public chat feature will help your wedding party get familiarized by allowing anyone to ask a question or make important announcements.

Events

No one in your wedding party will accidentally miss an event with our alert notifications and robust calendars. You're able to determine who sees which events, customize how and when alerts will be sent, and see everyone’s responses to invites.

Boards

Save and share all your wedding planning ideas in boards, from suits for the groomsmen to accessories and decor. With Pinterest integration, you can easily include boards you’ve already created for further inspiration.

We’ve even provided a board full of wedding tips for you and your wedding party to ease the process.

Multiple Weddings

Invited to more than one wedding, on top of planning your own? No problem - switching between weddings is a breeze, so you’ll be able to easily manage and track them all from the same place.
