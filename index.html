YOUR ALL IN ONE

I Do is a customizable app designed to help you plan and communicate with members of your wedding party all in one app without the hassle of having multiple platforms.

Make a Profile

Personalize your profile by adding important wedding information for everyone to see.

Invite Your Wedding Party

Easily find contacts from your phone, Facebook, and e-mails to invite them to join the app.

Assign Admins

Choose from your invites to add admins to help you plan and organize.

Messages

Don't worry about the hassle of getting everyone on the same messaging platform whether they're using IOS, Android, or prefer e-mailing. Our messaging platform keeps it simple and easy to use without all the clutter so you and your wedding party can focus on planning.

Our Public Chat feature allows anyone to ask a question or make important announcements to everyone in the wedding party. Plan faster with this feature by recieving answers quicker from anybody.

Events

No one in your wedding party will miss another scheduled event with our events alert notification. Create an event and share it with whomever you please and get automatic alerts or you can manually send out alerts. Sync any events you make to your personal calendar.

Boards

Save and share all your wedding planning from suits for the groomsmen to accessories and decor. Send URLs of where to buy products to your wedding party and keep everyone organized and ready for the big day.

Multiple Weddings

It's easier than ever before to add and change weddings. Whether you're planning your own wedding or are a part of many weddings, you can manage and keep track of them all right on the home screen.
